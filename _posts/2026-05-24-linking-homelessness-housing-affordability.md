What does it take to build a Nanaimo where everyone has a place to call home?

Join Strong Towns Nanaimo for an informal talk and discussion with **Dave Witty** of the Housing Nanaimo Working Group on the urgent link between homelessness and housing affordability. We'll explore why affordable and non-market housing are essential pieces of a healthy city, how market and non-market solutions can work together, and what it will take from government, non-profits, the private sector, and the broader community to get there.

* 📅 **Date:** Sunday, May 24, 2026  
* ⌚ **Time:** 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM PST  
* 📌 **Location:** Vancouver Island Regional Library, Downtown/Harbourfront Branch  
* ⌛ **Format:** Informal talk followed by open discussion  
* 🎫 **Admission:** All welcome
