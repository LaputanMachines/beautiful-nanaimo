## Nanaimo’s Missing Middle Gallery

Nanaimo’s best neighbourhoods aren’t homogeneous. They’re diverse, eclectic, varied, and textured. They feature housing of all shapes and sizes. They house young families and aging souls alike. This is a collaborative project with [Sidewalking Victoria](https://www.sidewalkingvictoria.com/) showcasing our city’s diverse housing types. You can click/tap on any photo to enlarge it.
